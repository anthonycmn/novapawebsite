✦ The Story

About the Show

“Let your freak flag fly!”

It’s a “big bright beautiful world” for everyone’s favorite ogre in Shrek The Musical, based on the Oscar-winning DreamWorks Animation film and the Tony Award-winning Broadway musical.

In a faraway kingdom, the green ogre Shrek finds his swamp invaded by banished fairytale misfits, runaways cast off by Lord Farquaad, a tiny terror with big ambitions. When Shrek sets off with a wisecracking donkey to confront Farquaad, he’s handed a task: if he rescues the feisty Princess Fiona from her dragon-guarded tower, his swamp will be returned to him.

Part romance and part twisted fairy tale, this irreverently fun show has a powerful message for the whole family — about friendship, loyalty, and embracing what makes you different.

About Broadway Bound

Broadway Bound is our flagship summer camp where young performers spend the season learning a full musical from start to finish — vocals, choreography, acting, and stagecraft — culminating in a live performance for friends and family. Learn more about camp & register →
