FAQs

What are the dates and daily hours?
Each camp is a two-week, full-day session, Monday–Friday from 8:30am to 4:00pm.
🐉 How to Train Your Dragon Jr. — July 5–16, 2027
🍫 Charlie & the Chocolate Factory Jr. — July 19–30, 2027
🌈 Trolls The Musical Jr. — August 2–13, 2027
What ages can attend?
Ages 5–15! Campers are grouped by age — 5–9, 9–12, and 12–15 — with a Technical Theatre track for ages 10–15. You'll pick your camper's age group during registration.
How much does camp cost?
$995 per two-week camp, with flexible payment plans available at checkout. Right now every camp is 10% off — and multi-camp families save more: 15% off each camp when you register for two, and 20% off each when you register for three. Discounts apply automatically at checkout through August 15th. One $25 annual family registration covers all children in your household.
Does my camper need theatre experience?
Not at all! Whether they're brand new to theatre or a seasoned performer, our experienced instructors meet every camper where they are — building confidence, skills, and friendships along the way.
Will there be a real performance?
Yes! Every session closes with fully staged performances for family and friends — costumes, sets, lights, the whole Broadway experience.
Where is camp held?
At the National Conference Center — 18980 Upper Belmont Place, Leesburg, VA 20176. Questions? info@novapa.org or (571) 571-2120.
