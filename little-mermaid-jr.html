✦ The Story

About the Show

“Up where they walk, up where they run…”

Journey “under the sea” with Ariel and her aquatic friends in Disney’s The Little Mermaid Jr. In a magical underwater kingdom, the beautiful young mermaid Ariel longs to leave her ocean home — and her fins — behind and live in the world above.

But first, she’ll have to defy her father, King Triton, make a deal with the cunning sea witch Ursula, and convince the handsome Prince Eric that she’s the girl whose enchanting voice he’s been searching for. With the help of Sebastian, Flounder, and Scuttle, Ariel discovers the sacrifices we all make for love and acceptance.

Based on one of Hans Christian Andersen’s most beloved stories and the classic animated film, the score features the Academy Award-winning music of Alan Menken with lyrics by Howard Ashman and Glenn Slater — including “Part of Your World,” “Under the Sea,” “Kiss the Girl,” and “Poor Unfortunate Souls.”

This 60-minute Junior Edition has been carefully adapted for young performers, written to flow seamlessly between land and sea — showcasing the range and energy of both our Kids (ages 5–9) and Junior (ages 9–12) casts.

A Note to Families

The Little Mermaid Jr. is a joyful, song-filled family musical that audiences of every age can enjoy together. The story’s villain, Ursula, brings a touch of playful menace, and younger viewers may find her scenes mildly intense — but the show ends with the bright, hopeful resolution families know and love.
