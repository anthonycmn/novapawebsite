✦ The Story

About the Show

“It’s a sad song… but we sing it anyway.”

Following two intertwining love stories — that of young dreamers Orpheus and Eurydice, and that of immortal King Hades and Lady Persephone — Hadestown invites audiences on a journey to the underworld and back.

Inspired by the traditions of classic American folk music and vintage New Orleans jazz, Anaïs Mitchell’s beguiling sung-through musical sets industry against nature, doubt against faith, and fear against love. It is a beautiful folk opera that delivers a deeply resonant, defiantly hopeful theatrical experience.

A gifted young musician, Orpheus risks everything to bring his love home from the depths of Hadestown — a story about hope, doubt, and the power of a song to change the world, told the way the world has been telling it for a very long time.

This Teen Edition is a full-length adaptation prepared by the original authors, with vocal arrangements transposed for younger voices and flexible, expandable ensemble casting — bringing the soul and spectacle of the Tony Award–winning musical to the NoVAPA stage.

A Note to Families

Hadestown: Teen Edition is a full-length adaptation prepared by the original authors for teen performers and family audiences. Language and staging related to substance use and the more mature references of the original production have been adjusted for this edition. The story still explores weighty themes — poverty, loss, and a journey to the underworld — with honesty and heart, and is generally recommended for ages 10 and up.
