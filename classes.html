Age-Based End Times

Ages 5–8 classes end by 7 PM weekdays. Ages 9–12 by 8 PM. Ages 13–15 by 8:30 PM. Ages 16–18 / DE program up to 10 PM.

Production Schedule

Broadway Bound Kids & Teens rehearse Wednesdays. Broadway Bound Jr. rehearses Tuesdays. All three companies have non-overlapping slots.

Season Dates

Classes run September 14, 2026 through June 11, 2027. Breaks include Thanksgiving Break (Nov 22–29), Winter Break (Dec 20–Jan 3), and Spring Break (Mar 22–26).

Student Safety & Staff Training

Create · Belong · Inspire

At NoVAPA, the safety, well-being, and dignity of every student is our highest priority. We understand that parents place tremendous trust in us when they enroll their children in our programs. We take that responsibility seriously, and we have established safety standards designed to create a secure, supportive, and positive learning environment for all participants.
