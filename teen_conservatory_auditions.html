2026–2027 Conservatory Productions

Three Shows. Infinite Possibilities.

Students may audition for one, two, or all three productions — and are warmly encouraged to be a part of the full conservatory year.

🗓 August 2026
Dear Evan Hansen

High School Version — A heartbreaking and hopeful story about finding connection in an age of social media. Opening the season.
🗓 Oct 23–25 & Oct 29 – Nov 1, 2026
Sweeney Todd

School Edition — Sondheim's thrilling, darkly comic masterpiece. The Demon Barber returns to Fleet Street.
🗓 March 5–21, 2027
Hadestown

Teen Edition — Anaïs Mitchell’s Tony Award–winning musical. A soulful retelling of the Orpheus and Eurydice myth on the road to the underworld. Closing the season.
What to Prepare

Audition Requirements

Prepare the following for both in-person and virtual auditions.

🎵
Song
1 Minute

One minute of a prepared song of your choice. Musical theatre strongly preferred but not required.

🎭
Monologue
30 Seconds

Thirty seconds of a prepared monologue. Any style welcome — dramatic, comedic, or contemporary.

Video Upload

Can't attend in person? Submit a video recording by June 14. Contact us for the submission link.

Students are welcome to audition for one, two, or all three shows — and we strongly encourage every conservatory student to be a part of the full season. The Conservatory is designed for dedicated performers who want to grow, challenge themselves, and build lasting friendships on stage.

Rehearsal Schedule

What to Expect

Conservatory students commit to the following weekly schedule:

Weekly Rehearsal Times

  • Saturday mornings — All conservatory students
  • Two nights per week — Evening rehearsals, 7:00 PM – 9:00 PM
  • Evening night selections based on student availability
  • Final schedule set collaboratively after auditions

Conservatory Commitment

  • This is an advanced, intensive training program
  • Students are encouraged to participate in all three productions
  • Build skills in acting, musical theatre, and ensemble performance
  • Work closely with professional directors and teaching artists
