The Weekend Schedule

Friday Night to Sunday Afternoon.

Every minute of this weekend is intentionally designed. Friday eases everyone in. Saturday is the deep work. Sunday is execution — the moment everything you’ve built becomes real.

Day One
Friday Evening
The Final Friday of August
5:30 PM – 9:00 PM
Student + Parent
Master Class Coaching on Materials
Bring your songs and monologues. Our coaches refine each piece so you walk into Saturday’s filming with audition-ready material.
“All About the College Process” Workshop for Parents
A dedicated workshop for parents covering timelines, school lists, prescreens vs. live auditions, financial considerations, and what to expect from Unifieds.
Live Q&A Session
Bring every question. Our coaching team answers everything from material selection to handling rejection.
Clothing & Audition Wardrobe Selection
Bring three outfits. We’ll help you select what works on camera and what flatters you for headshots, pre-screens, and the dance video.
Dance Polishing Session
Refine the choreography for your dance pre-screen with our dance coach so Saturday’s filming captures your best work.
Day Two
Saturday Intensive
The Final Saturday of August
9:00 AM – 7:00 PM
Student Only · Lunch Included
Individual Coaching on Every Piece
One-on-one coaching on each audition piece before it’s filmed. Every video gets a dedicated coach in the room.
Filming — Up to 3 Pre-Screen Videos
Professional videographers, professional lighting, professional audio. These are the videos you’ll submit to every program on your list.
Dance Pre-Screen Video
A filmed dance video for programs that require one — choreographed and coached on Friday, captured on Saturday.
Professional Headshot Session
Studio-quality headshots taken by our photographer — suitable for every application portal and showcase.
Resume Workshop & Build
Build a professional performance resume from scratch — or polish the one you have to industry standards.
Common App & GetAcceptd Portal Setup
Set up accounts, understand each portal, learn how to upload pre-screens, and complete the foundation of your applications.
Essay Coaching with a Dedicated Coach
Brainstorm, draft, and refine your audition essay with our essay coach. By the end of Saturday, you’ll have one essay nearly complete.
Catered Lunch
A real meal so you can stay focused and energized through a long, transformative day.
Day Three
Sunday Launch
The Final Sunday of August
9:00 AM – 1:00 PM
Student Only
Final Video Review & Approvals
Watch every edited pre-screen with your coach. Approve them. Done.
Complete Your Second Essay
Finish what you started Saturday, then write a second essay so you leave with at least two polished pieces ready for submission.
Live Submission to Your First College
The moment of truth. You’ll submit your first complete application with us in the room — learning the exact process to repeat for every other school on your list.

Who This Is For

Rising Seniors
Ready to Launch.

This weekend is designed for rising high school seniors auditioning for BFA, BA, and conservatory programs in Musical Theatre, Acting, or Dance.

  • ✓
    You have a working list of 5–10 schools (or are ready to build one with us).
  • ✓
    You’ve started selecting some audition material (songs, monologues, choreography), even if it’s rough.
  • ✓
    You’re ready to work hard for three days — this is not a passive weekend.
  • ✓
    You want to start the audition season early, ahead of the fall scramble.
